 As I see it, currently an admins job would be:<br>
<br>
- PK and /image<br>
- buying/selling items (if you want)<br>
- giving quests (if you want)<br>
- settling disputes (if interference is warranted, perhaps the players should be free to @#%$ themselves over if they desire)<br>
<br>
IE, not overly much. As the world is majorly player driven, we are lacking things to do.<br>
<br>
In fact, im sure it wouldnt be overly hard to make it cost, say, 200 gold per image change and have a NPC to do it. And/or, a PK command the players can use which will change their PK status after a set amount of time (so you dont have players changing their status all the time to aviod getting killed).<br>
<br>
This way, we could limit the intrusion of admins into the players lives.
